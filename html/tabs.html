Even for a project like this, Tabs Immortalized is still on the sillier end of things. I often have many browser tabs open on my computer at any time. The way I use them is similar to how I would use sticky notes: As a temporary space to remind myself of topics that, while not important enough to put on a calendar or a notebook, I would like to remember nonetheless. So here we are. In an attempt to clean up my browser tabs and also to stick with the theme of providing a living space for emphemeral thoughts, here we have Tabs Immortalized.
